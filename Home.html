Welcome to

STRATFORD 

Market Center

We're so glad you're here. We're a family-owned and operated deli that's been serving up delicious food to our community for over 3 years. We're passionate about food, and we're committed to providing our customers with the freshest, highest-quality ingredients possible.

Whether you're looking for a quick bite to eat on the go or a full meal to enjoy at home, we have something for everyone. We also offer a variety of convenient services, such as online ordering and delivery..

Vegetables

Our fresh fruit and vegetables are sourced from local farmers whenever possible. We have a wide variety of fruits and vegetables to choose from, including apples, oranges, bananas, tomatoes, cucumbers, and lettuce.

Home Made Salad

Our homemade salads are made with fresh, seasonal produce. We have a variety of salads to choose from, including classic Caesar salad, cobb salad, and garden salad.

Sandwiches

Our sandwiches are made with Boar's Head meats and cheeses, which are known for their high quality and delicious flavor. We have a variety of sandwiches to choose from, including classic turkey and Swiss, ham and cheddar, and roast beef and provolone.

Fried Chicken

Our fried chicken is made with the freshest ingredients and cooked to perfection. The chicken is juicy and flavorful, and the breading is crispy and golden brown.
